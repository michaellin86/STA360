Find the stationary distribution $\pi$ of transition matrix $T$:
\begin{center}
$T = 
\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 0 & 1/2 & 0 & 1/2 \\
0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\
1/4 & 1/4 & 0 & 1/4 & 1/4 \\
0 & 0 & 1/2 & 0 & 1/2 \\
0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$
\end{center}

\textbf{Answer:} $\pi=
\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$

\textbf{Solution:} Compute $\lim\limits_{n\to \infty} T^n$ and read off the first row.
